Core Technology

The Metal-Alloy Ingot Sponge

Our flagship technology stores hydrogen within the crystal lattice of a proprietary metal-alloy sponge at just 8–10 bar — a fraction of the 880-bar operating pressure required by compressed gas systems. The result: vessels that are safer, lighter, and manufacturable with standard industrial equipment.

Unlike cryogenic storage, our system operates at near-ambient conditions. Unlike compressed gas, it achieves 5–8 wt% hydrogen density — far exceeding alternatives in the solid-state category.

  • No cryogenic infrastructure required
  • Dramatically reduced vessel wall thickness
  • Compatible with existing hydrogen supply chains
  • Scalable from portable to industrial volumes
  • Reversible absorption and desorption cycle

Applications

Built for every industry hydrogen serves.

01

Mobility & Fleet

Solid-state storage is purpose-built for hydrogen fuel-cell vehicles. Lower operating pressure means simpler tank geometry, reduced engineering overhead, and inherently safer transport. Our systems are designed for passenger vehicles, commercial trucks, and fleet applications where refueling infrastructure is co-located.

02

Metallurgy

We target the industrial hydrogen ecosystem — steel producers, chemical processors, and distributed energy operators — where delivery cost and storage density directly affect margins. Our systems replace high-pressure tube trailers with safer, denser alternatives that reduce total cost of ownership over the asset lifecycle.

03

Energy

Hydrogen is the feedstock for ammonia synthesis and the global fertilizer supply. Bringing affordable, green hydrogen storage to the agricultural sector represents one of the largest decarbonization opportunities available. Our solid-state systems enable on-site or near-site hydrogen storage without the safety burden of compressed gas.

Who We Are

American-built hydrogen technology for the industries that power tomorrow.

Phoebus American Energy Systems is a Worcester, Massachusetts-based startup developing next-generation metal-alloy sponge technology for solid-state hydrogen storage. Founded by engineers and materials scientists, we exist to make hydrogen a practical, deployable fuel for the industries that compressed gas has failed to serve.

Our work is grounded in rigorous materials science — XRD, SEM, TEM, and hands-on alloy development — and our technology is protected under provisional patent #63/863,916. We are a team that builds in the lab and thinks in systems.

What We're Building

Rethinking how hydrogen is stored, moved, and used.

The industrial hydrogen market has been dominated by compressed gas since the 1800s. We think that's about to change.

Core Technology

Solid-State Metal-Alloy Storage

Our flagship technology stores hydrogen within the crystal lattice of a proprietary metal-alloy sponge at just 8–10 bar — a fraction of the 880-bar operating pressure required by compressed gas systems. The result: vessels that are safer, lighter, and manufacturable with standard industrial equipment.

  • No cryogenic infrastructure required
  • Dramatically reduced vessel wall thickness
  • Compatible with existing hydrogen supply chains
  • Scalable from portable to industrial volumes
Applications

Mobile & Fleet

Solid-state storage is purpose-built for hydrogen fuel-cell vehicles. Lower operating pressure means simpler tank geometry, reduced engineering overhead, and inherently safer transport.

Applications

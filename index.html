Who We Are

American-built hydrogen technology for the industries that power tomorrow.

Phoebus American Energy Systems is a Worcester, Massachusetts-based startup developing next-generation metal-alloy sponge technology for solid-state hydrogen storage. Founded by engineers and materials scientists, we exist to make hydrogen a practical, deployable fuel.

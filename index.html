Who We Are

America's next generation

Phoebus American Energy Systems is a Worcester, Massachusetts- based startup company developing metal-alloy sponge technology for solid-state hydrogen storage. Founded by a group of engineers, scientists and businessmen, Phoebus exists to make hydrogen a practical, deployable fuel for virtually any application.
